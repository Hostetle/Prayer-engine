The 10 Commandments 

You can read the 10 Commandments in the Bible in Exodus 20:3-17 I'm going to translate them into today's vernacular.

Thou shalt have no other gods before me. Make Jesus the highest priority in your life.

Thou shalt not make unto thee any graven image. Statue of a god you would worship or expect answers from.

Thou shalt not bow down thyself to any man made god, or statue.

Thou shalt not take the name of the Lord thy God in vain. Don't use the name of Jesus or God to swear with.

Remember the sabbath day, to keep it Holy. Take a day off to rest and honour God each week.

Honor they father and mother that thy days may be long upon the land which the Lord thy God giveth thee.

Thou shalt not kill, commit murder.

Thou shalt not commit adultery, get involved with another person after marriage.

Thou shalt not steal.

Thou shalt not bear false witness against thy neighbour. Don't tell a lie about other people you know for any reason.

Thou shalt not covet thy neighbour's house, marriage partner, car, truck, or anything your neighbor has that you like.
